Research

I am an Assistant Professor at Harvard's Department of Psychology. I head the Computation, Cognition, and Development lab, with a focus on intuitive theories and people's common-sense reasoning about physics and psychology. I'm also a member of the Center for Brains, Minds and Machines, and an affiliate of the Kempner Institute for the Study of Natural and Artificial Intelligence.
