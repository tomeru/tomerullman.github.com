Research

I am a postdoctoral associate in the Computational Cognitive Science group at MIT, and the Lab for Developmental Studies at Harvard. I study people's common-sense reasoning about physics and psychology, using computational models and behavioral experiments with children and adults. My research is funded by the Center for Brains, Minds and Machines.
